About Us

Our Mission

At V-Phone, our mission is to revolutionize how people buy and sell smartphones. We aim to provide a safe, fast, and reliable platform that connects buyers and sellers from across Indonesia. With a strong focus on technology and customer experience, we are building a digital marketplace that empowers individuals to easily exchange smartphones in a secure and transparent environment. We are committed to reshaping the second-hand smartphone industry by making transactions not only easier but also more enjoyable for everyone involved.

Our Values

Trust: Every transaction on V-Phone is protected by a strict verification system to ensure safety and prevent fraud.

Innovation: We continuously develop new features to enhance user convenience, from smart product filters to integrated payment systems.

Transparency: Prices, product conditions, and seller reputations are always displayed openly.

Sustainability: We support the circular economy by making it easy for users to resell their still-usable devices.

Our Approach

At V-Phone, we combine cutting-edge technology with a simple and user-friendly interface to make buying and selling phones as smooth as possible. Listing a phone for sale is quick and effortless — users just need to snap a photo, write a short description, and their product is ready to reach thousands of potential buyers.

To enhance communication and trust, we provide a built-in live chat feature, allowing buyers and sellers to interact directly and clarify details before completing a transaction. In addition, our dedicated customer support team is available 24/7, ready to assist users with any questions or issues they may face throughout the transaction process.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, V-Phone began as a simple idea by a group of passionate tech enthusiasts and young entrepreneurs who saw a major gap in the second-hand smartphone market in Indonesia. At the time, the market was disorganized, risky, and lacked a centralized, reliable platform.

Starting from a basic mobile application, V-Phone quickly gained traction by focusing on user trust, convenience, and innovation. Over the years, we have evolved into Indonesia’s leading smartphone marketplace, facilitating thousands of secure and successful transactions each month.

Today, V-Phone continues to grow, driven by our mission to make second-hand smartphone buying and selling smarter, more secure, and accessible to everyone.
